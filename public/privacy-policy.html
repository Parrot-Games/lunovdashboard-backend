Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Lunov (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our Discord bot, website, or related services.

1. Information We Collect

When using Lunov, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Server Data: Server IDs, channel IDs, and role information to enable configuration and commands.
  • User Data: User IDs, usernames, and message content (only when necessary for moderation or logging features).
  • Usage Data: Command usage statistics, error reports, and system logs to improve performance and reliability.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and improve Lunov’s performance and functionality.
  • To provide customized features such as moderation logs, prefix settings, and analytics.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, spam, or service misuse.
  • To communicate updates or important information related to the bot or services.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data collected is securely stored on our servers and is not shared with third parties except as required by law. We use appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification of stored data.

4. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to operate Lunov and maintain essential functionality. Server data is deleted upon request or when a server removes Lunov.

5. Third-Party Services

Lunov interacts with Discord’s API and may rely on third-party hosting services (such as Render) to maintain uptime. These services may process limited data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Cookies and Website Data

Our website may use cookies for analytics or user preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function correctly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your data. To make a data request, contact us through our support server.

8. Children’s Privacy

Lunov does not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us via our Discord support server.
